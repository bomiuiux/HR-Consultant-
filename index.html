About Us

HR Experts Who've Been in the Trenches

We founded PeoplePath after watching brilliant founders make avoidable, costly mistakes with hiring, compliance, and culture. We've sat on both sides of the table — and we built this consultancy specifically for companies navigating the zero-to-one and one-to-ten journeys.
